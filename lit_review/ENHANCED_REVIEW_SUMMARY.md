## Key Improvements Over Original Review

### 1. **Convergence to Real Gaps**

The enhanced review identifies **three convergent gaps** that define a clear research agenda:

#### Gap 1: The Permanent Crop Gap
- **Magnitude**: Only 2.9% of AI studies target permanent crops (olives)
- **Impact**: Despite olives covering 65% of Morocco's fruit tree area
- **Research Priority**: CNN-LSTM architectures for permanent crop stress detection

#### Gap 2: The Granularity Gap  
- **Magnitude**: 100-fold spatial resolution deficit (regional >1 km² vs. plot-scale <100 m²)
- **Impact**: Current models fail to support precision irrigation
- **Research Priority**: GEE-based multi-sensor fusion for plot-scale detection

#### Gap 3: The Actionability Gap
- **Magnitude**: Only 5.9% of studies integrate socio-economic factors
- **Impact**: Technically accurate models are socially unimplementable
- **Research Priority**: XAI frameworks + economic data integration + operational deployment
